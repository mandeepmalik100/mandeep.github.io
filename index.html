Mandeep Malik

I am pursuing doctoral studies at Indian Institute of Information Technology, Nagpur in Wireless Communication working with Prof. Ashwin Kothari.

I completed Master in Technology (Communication Systems) from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Roorkee and presented thesis on RF Energy Harvesting under the supervision of Prof. Debashis Ghosh.

My research interests are in Low Powered Wide Area Networks (LPWAN), Nb-IoT, LTE CAT-M, 5G Core Network and Ng-RAN. In particular, I have worked on optimizing resource allocation, infrastructure placement, and wireless network parameters in large-scale networks and edge deployments..
